Field


Emergence is the arising of novel and coherent structures, patterns and properties during the process of self-organization in complex systems
- Jeffrey Goldstein

Emergent behaviour in dynamical systems with complex rules inspired by the physics of attraction and replusion resemble flocking and swarming but repeated random snapshots of one state of the system suggest a delibrate arrangement.

The information flow in a field creates a network which gives rise to new global patterns and tendencies, and a field becomes more than a collection of particles.
