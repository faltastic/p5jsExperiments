New Color


We see emergence when termites build cathedrals with elaborate architecture, when birds flock in what looks like rehearsed choreography, when neural activity in the brain give birth to intelligence and consciousness, and when pixels in images are sorted and rearranged to produce these visual compositions.

The common observation here is that new high-level features of the system as a whole are born out of the low-level properties of the individual components. As a decentralized system grows, its internal processes may turn into self-organization and the unexpected may evolve.

The snapshots of the pixel dynamics in this collection were selected to reflect an ambience of design and artistic expression. We see a reflection of our own human emergent properties in what the system produces. In effect, we anthropomorphize it.
